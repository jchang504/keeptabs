Daily tasks

List here tasks that you need to complete every day, but don't want to create calendar events for. Their duration will be counted for each day when calculating free time. If a daily task's name is contained in the title of a scheduled event, the duration of the daily task will not be counted for the day of that event. (E.g. when you schedule "Lunch with Bill" your "lunch" daily task duration won't be counted that day.) When computing free time for today, if the current time is past a daily task's completed by time, the daily task's duration won't be counted.

Calculation options

Leeway is the ideal amount of unallocated time you want per day. Short event duration is the duration you want short (≤ 40 minutes) events to be approximated as.* Transition time is the amount of allocated time automatically added for each regular (> 40 minutes) event. Specify all amounts in minutes.

* The exact duration can't be computed because the event's box is so small that the end time doesn't show. This hack will be fixed in the future.
